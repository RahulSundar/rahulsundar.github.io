# Dhyuti Frontiers
*[Visit dhyutifrontiers.info](https://dhyutifrontiers.info)*

A community initiative to enable capacity building & knowledge dissemination in AI for Science and Engineering. Dhyuti enables knowledge sharing through various interactive seminar formats and encourages open source contributions from budding SciML researchers through [DhyutiLABS](https://github.com/DhyutiLABS).
